Profile is Me

I am an IT university student, and like many others who are still learning, my code is often full of bugs. Sometimes it feels frustrating to see errors pop up again and again, and it can be disheartening when things don't work the way I expect them to. But I've learned that this is all part of the journey. Every bug, every failed build, and every crash is a valuable lesson. I choose not to give up. Instead, I see each challenge as an opportunity to grow. I spend time reading documentation, asking questions, and experimenting with different solutions. I might not get it right the first time—or even the fifth—but I never stop learning.
